  The Wiko's touchscreen is dead.
Today's objective is to get inside the device, to regain access and eventually add it to the list of available devices I can play with. 

Attempt 1 : Touchscreen Transplant

First idea: solder a touchscreen flex cable directly onto the phone.
The operation fails.
No response. The Wiko stays silent. 

Attempt #2 — Bypassing touch by Using ADB

  Second strategy: create an ADB (Android Debug Bridge) environment to control the phone from a computer. 


  In this case, the phone refuses the role switch.

Attempt #3 - OTG mouse test

A mouse plugged into the phone using an OTG cable


  The plan is to build a  USB-OTG cable , plug in a mouse, and unlock the interface without relying on touch. Another dead end. The Wiko does not support USB-OTG.
